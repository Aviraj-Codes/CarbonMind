# Carbon Mind - Personal Carbon Footprint Analyzer

A modern, interactive web application that helps you understand and reduce your environmental impact through personalized carbon analysis and actionable recommendations.

## Features

- **Real-time Carbon Analysis**: Calculate your carbon footprint across travel, diet, and energy consumption
- **Behavioral Clustering**: AI-powered lifestyle categorization with sustainability scoring
- **Personalized Recommendations**: Get 8 tailored reduction strategies with impact estimates
- **Impact Simulation**: Visualize your potential carbon reduction before/after applying changes
- **Responsive Design**: Seamlessly works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Light/Dark Mode**: Comfortable viewing in any lighting condition
- **Interactive Charts**: Beautiful visualizations of your emissions breakdown

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: Next.js 16, React 19, TypeScript
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion
- **Visualization**: Recharts
- **Deployment**: Vercel

## How It Works

1. **Input Your Habits**: Enter details about your weekly travel, diet, and energy usage
2. **Click Calculate**: Process your data through our carbon calculation engine
3. **View Results**: See your carbon score, emissions breakdown, and behavioral profile
4. **Explore Recommendations**: Review personalized reduction strategies
5. **Simulate Impact**: Run a simulation to see potential emissions reduction

## Carbon Calculation Methodology

- **Travel**: Based on weekly car/transit/flight usage with vehicle type factors
- **Food**: Calculated from diet type with consumption and waste multipliers
- **Energy**: Derived from electricity usage, AC hours, and renewable energy percentage

Emission factors are based on industry-standard carbon accounting practices.

## Behavioral Clustering

The app automatically categorizes your lifestyle into clusters:

- **Green Guardian**: Strong environmental commitment
- **Car Dependent**: High vehicle usage impact
- **Meat Lover**: High-impact diet choices
- **Energy Consumer**: Significant energy usage
- **Balanced Lifestyle**: Moderate impact across categories
